ABOUT ME

Hello everyone, I am Nurmatjonov Javokhir. I live in Tashkent. I study at the specialized school for the advanced study of information and communication technologies named after Muhammad al-Khwarizmi. I am interested in IT. Maybe that's why I chose this school. During my early childhood I wanted to become a military man, an artist, a gamer, a business man; but at the same time I felt passion towards communication technologies and computers. I developed the interest in information technology by playing computer games and typing on the keyboard. I caught myself thinking about the gaming process instead of enjoying games like my peers do. Why does a function work after pressing a button? How does the system work? How does AI functioning? Then I started to watch different kind of video on youtube and got subscribed many IT bloggers and gradually went deeper and deeper into this field. My father is actively participating in my development, education and career. He introduced me Computer Academy when I was 13. This school changed my life completely. I gained more skills and knowledge about computers, programming and IT in general. Thanks to my school I got a lot of opportunities to improve in information technologies. Robotics is one of my favorite specialties which I have keen interest In and acquired high achievements and awards.
